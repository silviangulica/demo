Donation FAQ

Answers to commonly asked questions about donations

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, we are a registered charity and all donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for your donation that can be used for tax purposes.

Can I specify how my donation is used?

Absolutely! You can designate your donation for a specific project, entrepreneur, or program area. Just add a note during the donation process or contact us directly.

How do I cancel or modify my monthly donation?

You can cancel or modify your recurring donation at any time by logging into your donor account or by contacting our donor support team at support@charityforentrepreneurs.org.

Can I donate in memory or honor of someone?

Yes, we offer memorial and tribute giving options. You can provide details during the donation process, and we'll send an acknowledgment card to the person of your choice.
